This folder contains **five fully-realized theme mockups** for the Desert Boy Ministries of Jesus website. Each mockup is a complete, interactive HTML page that demonstrates visual design, layout, user experience enhancements, and engagement features tailored to the Christian + desert motif.

## üé® The Five Themes

### 1. **Sands of Grace** - Warm Desert Minimalism
**File:** `theme1-sands-of-grace.html`

**Concept:** Clean, minimalist design with warm sand tones (beiges, ochres, soft golds), ample white space, and modern sans-serif typography.

**Key Features:**
- Full-width hero section with desert dunes imagery
- Sticky navigation that shrinks on scroll
- Terracotta accent colors for CTAs
- Featured message section with elevated card design
- Sand-grain texture backgrounds
- Parallax scroll effects

**Best For:** Ministries wanting clarity, simplicity, and a calming aesthetic that lets content shine.

---

### 2. **Oasis of Worship** - Rich Colors + Christian Symbolism
**File:** `theme2-oasis-of-worship.html`

**Concept:** Vibrant palette (turquoise, sunset purples/oranges, gold) with Christian symbolism (cross, dove, rays of light) representing hope, refreshment, and revival.

**Key Features:**
- Animated hero with symbolic overlays (cross, dove)
- Testimonies slider showcasing transformation stories
- Interactive journey map with ministry milestones
- "Light & Life" blog highlight section
- Rich jewel-tone backgrounds

**Best For:** Engaging younger audiences and seekers with energy, hope, and visual storytelling.

---

### 3. **Desert Mission** - Documentary / Story-Driven Layout
**File:** `theme3-desert-mission.html`

**Concept:** Magazine-style layout with large featured photographs, immersive full-width image blocks, and authentic mission storytelling.

**Key Features:**
- Scroll-story sections with parallax effects
- Outreach projects grid with hover overlays
- "Meet the Team" interactive profiles
- Earthy, textured backgrounds (rust, olive, sand)
- Documentary-style photography focus

**Best For:** Mission-focused ministries wanting to showcase real-life impact and field stories.

---

### 4. **Modern Desert Church** - Clean Tech-Forward Design
**File:** `theme4-modern-desert-church.html`

**Concept:** Contemporary design with flat design principles, generous whitespace, animated micro-interactions, and light/dark mode toggle.

**Key Features:**
- **Light/Dark mode toggle** (fully functional)
- Animated counters for social proof (mission trips, countries reached)
- Hide/show navigation on scroll
- Live chat widget for engagement
- Modern geometric fonts and clean layouts
- Newsletter signup section

**Best For:** Tech-savvy, digital-native audiences (Gen Z/Millennials) and forward-thinking ministries.

---

### 5. **Heritage & Horizon** - Traditional Faith + Expansive Vision
**File:** `theme5-heritage-horizon.html`

**Concept:** Blend of traditional church aesthetics (serif fonts, classic layouts, burgundy/royal blue accents) with sweeping desert horizon imagery.

**Key Features:**
- Interactive timeline component showing ministry history
- Classic sermon series archive with elegant cards
- Invitation banner: "Come as you are ‚Äî join us under the open sky"
- Traditional typography (Crimson Text, Playfair Display)
- Three-column footer with comprehensive links

**Best For:** Ministries wanting to honor tradition while casting vision for the future.

     - `theme1-sands-of-grace.html`

     - `theme3-desert-mission.html`
     - `theme4-modern-desert-church.html`
     - `theme5-heritage-horizon.html`

### Interactive Features

- **Theme 4 (Modern Desert Church)** has a working light/dark mode toggle button
- All themes have hover effects on buttons, cards, and navigation
- Responsive design works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- All mockups use only HTML/CSS/vanilla JavaScript (no external dependencies)

### Presenting to Your DevOps Team

1. **Share the entire `theme-mockups` folder** with your team
2. **Start with `index.html`** for the overview and comparison
3. **Let them explore each theme** individually
4. **Discuss which elements** from each theme resonate most with your vision
5. **Mix and match features** - you don't have to choose just one theme!
